Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of work does Top Home Renovations handle?

Just about everything inside and outside your home — painting, drywall and tile, electrical and plumbing fixtures, flooring, furniture assembly, TV mounting, gutter cleaning, general repairs, and full remodels. If you're not sure we cover it, just call (281) 827-3033 and ask.

Are you licensed and insured?

We are fully insured and a recognized Thumbtack Top Pro — among the highest-rated, most-booked pros on the platform, backed by 84 five-star reviews. Every job is treated with care and respect for your home.

How fast can you come out?

We're available 24/7 and can often schedule same-day or next-day, 7 days a week. Tell us what you need when you reach out and we'll find a window that works for you.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. We'll look at the job, explain your options in plain English, and give you a clear price before any work begins — no surprises. Call (281) 827-3033 or use the form above.

Do you take on small jobs, or only big projects?

Both. We're just as happy hanging a wall clock, reattaching a dishwasher, or patching drywall as we are remodeling a bathroom. Many customers have us knock out a whole to-do list in one visit.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Lilburn and serve the surrounding Gwinnett and DeKalb communities — including Lawrenceville, Snellville, Tucker, Stone Mountain, Norcross, Duluth, Grayson, and Lithonia. Not sure if you're in our area? Just call and we'll let you know.

Do you offer financing or take cards?

Yes. We offer flexible financing on larger projects and accept card payments, so it's easy to move forward on the work your home needs.
